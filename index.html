blackjack

rule

  • This game is played with 3 decks of 52 cards, and players will start with $100
  • Aces can be counted as 1 or 11 points
  • Cards 2-10 count according to their face value
  • J, Q, K count as 10 points
  • Player starts with two cards and play against the dealer
  • One of the dealer's cards will be face down
  • Player can "Stand" to end your turn or "Hit" to ask for another card
  • Dealer reveals hidden card after player's turn and if the dealer draws total of 16 or less, dealer will draw another card
  • If your total value exceeds 21, you "bust" and lose the game
  • If the dealer's value is closer to 21, you lose and vice versa
  • If the dealer busts, any player whho didn't bust wins
  • If you have 21 total you have Blackjack and automatically win unless the dealer has same cards as you called "push"
  • There are no split in the game
